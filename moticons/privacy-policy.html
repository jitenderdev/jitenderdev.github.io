Privacy Policy

  • Download Moticons

Moticons do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or credit card. It can store file names and paths locally to display them in history lists. It can store other configuration that could improve your experiencie when using the application. Moticons do not transfer this stored locally information outside of device.

Moticons use following permissions:

  • BILLING: to implement purchases to disable advertising and buy Moticoins.
  • VIBRATE: to allow a simple vibration when a Moticon is selected.
  • INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE and READ_PHONE_STATE: to allow advertising to work.
